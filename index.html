I live in Calgary and spend most of my free time with my sister. She has 2 kids that are age 17 & 15.

Calgary is a good city - particularly for families. It's divided up into residential communities and there are many parks and cycling trails.

Did I mention the beautiful Bow River runs right through Calgary?

Vancouver is a great city here in Canada, as well.

I lived there for 11 years, from 1994 through to 2005. I have so many goiod memories of living on the Canadian West Coast.

My favorite place to retreat and gain perspective was Jericho Beach on the West Side of Vancouver.

The real estate prices in this area of Vancouver started around, low end, $2 M for a small house. It's a very desireable area of vancouver to live in.

I walked, biked and drove up Spanish Banks by Jericho many times. Spanish Banks lead up to the University of British Columbia.

This image brings back so many memories and feelings. I can almost feel the gravel under my feel now.

I used to put on headphones and walk these trails when I lived on 4th Avenue in Vancouver. Almost feels like it was one of the best times of my life!

I paid $1050 / month for a place close to Jericho Beach and I realize now how fortunate I was to be living in such a beautiful place.
